Corporate Events

At SoundtechGoa we provide services exclusively for corporate events such as Conferences, Seminars, Product Launches, Exhibitions, gala balls, award nights to media wrap and office parties.

We provide entire technical production for your event, including design, customized fabrication, build and engineering services for your sound and lighting requirements, as well as any staging and AV elements.

We believe our success is down to working closely to the client’s brief to create and deliver the right atmosphere for your event, at the highest professional level.

  • Lighting shows

  • AV projection, screens & LED video walls

  • Staging & dance floors

  • Pyrotechnics & special effects

  • Full DJ & equipment packages
