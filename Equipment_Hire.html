Equipment Hire

From a corporate presentation to a nightclub promotion, a private function to a fashion show – SoundtechGoa has the expertise and technical knowledge to help you stage a successful and memorable event, throughout the process you are assured of complete confidence and peace of mind.

SoundtechGoa offers a professional hire service supplying portable DJ, sound, lighting, staging and AV equipment, and systems for both domestic and commercial use.

We hold a full inventory of equipment from compact packaged PA and lighting systems for a private party, through to full stage sound reinforcement and lighting for larger corporate events.

We can arrange collection / delivery, and expert crew to set-up the equipment hired.
